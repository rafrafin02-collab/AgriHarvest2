Crop Yield & Losses

Crop Yield

  • Depends on soil fertility and irrigation
  • Improved seeds increase production
  • Proper fertilizer boosts growth
  • Modern techniques improve efficiency

Crop Losses

  • Pests and diseases damage crops
  • Flood and drought cause major losses
  • Improper harvesting leads to wastage
  • Poor storage increases loss

Minimizing Losses

  • Use pest-resistant seeds
  • Apply proper irrigation methods
  • Harvest crops at the right time
  • Improve storage facilities
